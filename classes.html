Cooking Instruction

Want to learn how to make inspired vegan dishes to impress all your friends? Book a cooking class with Sumac + Sage. Teresa will work with you to design a menu, brings everything needed to make the class happen, teaches tips and tricks around the kitchen, and takes all the dirty dishes when she leaves.

Whether you've been vegan for 20 years, or are just starting out, Teresa has a wealth of knowledge to share. Teresa personalizes the experience so you walk away with not only one dish, but the recipes and knowledge of how to make all the components that you can mix and match with other meals of your own.

How to Book a Class

To book a class, simply reach out to Teresa via Instagram @sumac.and.sage, or send her a message, and she will reach back out to you. You will set up a phone consultation so Teresa to finalize dates, and she will work with you to come up with a plan for what you want to learn to make. Teresa offers classes in the Cincinnati region and will travel as far as Columbus in any direction. All dishes must use oil and salt but can be modified for allergens or food preferences.
